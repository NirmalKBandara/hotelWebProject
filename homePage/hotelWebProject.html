OCEAN LINE HOTELS

Where the waves meet luxury, and your journey finds a home.

— COASTAL CHARM

Overlooking some of the world’s most stunning beaches, Ocean Line Hotels stands as the epitome of beachside luxury. Here, you will be treated to mesmerizing views of azure waters and vibrant coastal landscapes. Spanning over 10 acres of beautifully manicured grounds, our hotel features 300 lavish rooms and suites. A short walk will take you to lively cultural hubs, bustling markets, and iconic landmarks.

— HIGHLIGHTS

Ocean Line Hotels is ideally located along the serene beachfronts, offering the perfect balance of relaxation and accessibility. Just steps away from vibrant local attractions and conveniently situated 30 kilometers from the international airport, it’s the ideal destination for both leisure and business travelers.
